IMPORTANT REMINDER!!


- The best hiking weather is warm and breezy but not too hot or windy.
but cold days are also a pleasure to hike in if you wear multiple layers
of clothing to regulate your body temperature and avoid sweating.



Make sure you carry all of the necessary essentials with you including
appropriate clothing, water, snacks and a compass. Be weather wise. Keep
an eye on the weather forecast and know the signs of an approaching storm.
